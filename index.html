About Me

Leslie grew up in Dallas surrounded by the arts. She originally attended Southern Methodist University as a music major, but because she also has other passions, she switched her majors to Cinema-Television and Psychology and graduated with two Bachelor of Arts

In 2010, Leslie graduated from New York University with a Masters in Children's Media, where she studied television and other electronic technology as educational tools for children and their benefits. Since her time at NYU, Leslie has been developing a children's television show targeted at 4-8 year old children to help develop their music literacy when the don't have the ability to gain this in other environments.

For about 9 years, Leslie was employed at KIDZ BOP, the number one selling CD for children, where she served as the head of Social Media and Online Community. In her spare time, Leslie enjoys puppeteering her puppet, Bleeckie, spending time with her family and friends, and walking her dog, Leia. She is beyond excited to learn more about coding through SMU's Coding Bootcamp in order to advance her career and her creativity!
